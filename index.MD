# Privacy Policy — Chore Quest

**Last updated: June 2025**

## Overview
Chore Quest is a children's chore-tracking game for families. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data We Collect
**We collect no personal data.** Chore Quest stores all information locally on your device only:
- Child's name and pet name
- Chore list and completion history
- Game scores and progress
- App settings (language, PIN, sounds)

This data never leaves your device. There is no account system, no backend server, and no internet connection required to use the app.

## In-App Purchases
Chore Quest offers optional tip/donation purchases processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)
Chore Quest is designed for children ages 6–12, used together with a parent. We do not:
- Collect any personal information from children
- Share any data with third parties
- Use advertising or analytics SDKs
- Require an account or login

## Third-Party Services
The app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Contact
Questions? Email us: workforpica@gmail.com

## Changes
If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely.
